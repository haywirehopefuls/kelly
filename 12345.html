As we transition from summer to fall–  the gorgeous weather, vibrant tree-lined streets, and all the festivities make October a magical month. There’s just something about pumpkin patches and Friday night lights that give the cozy fall vibes. But this October, there are more than football games and corn mazes to celebrate this spectacular season– and we began the second week with International Beer and Pizza Day!  

A delicious slice of pizza paired with a tasty craft beer is the perfect combination; I’ve got a couple of doughy places you might want to visit so you can enjoy one of the most internationally embraced traditions! 


The Front Door Taphouse 
If you’re craving the perfect combo of hot pizza and cold beer, you will want to add The Front Door Taphouse to the list! Not only do they make “flavorful and inventive pizza creations,” they are partnered with microbreweries in the area to make sure their taps are always flowing with the latest local beer!


Flying Pie
From their habanero pizza to their Triple Pi – Belgian-style Abbey ale, the Flying Pie has been an iconic pizza destination in the Treasure Valley since 1978! 
For a more in-depth list of where you can experience the tasty duo of a cheesy slice and a hazy pint, check out this week’s blog! I would love to hear where you go when you are craving pizza!
